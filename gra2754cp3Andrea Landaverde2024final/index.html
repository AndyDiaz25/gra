DIY Your Own Paper Beads

Materials

  • Paper Scraps
  • Cocktail Straw
  • Mod Podge Gloss
  • Scotch Tape

Tools

  • Craft knife and mat
  • Rulers
  • Scissors
  • Paintbrush

How to Make Paper Beads

Measure and cut strips of 12 inch narrow triangles

Place tape on the wide end of the triangle and put a straw on the tape

Add dabs of glue on the paper and roll the paper

Once rolled, smooth the bead to secure and let it dry.

Once dried, add 2 coats with mod podge and let it dry.

Admire your paper beads and get creative with them.
